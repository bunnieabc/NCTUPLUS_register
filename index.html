Introduction

Form of Symposium

In addition to conventions and meetings in the Symposium for important issues, courses will provided for participants, including Chinese medical treatments lessons. Activities such as the post-symposium trip will be organized to boost interaction between representatives of countries as well as to experience Taiwan's culture and spectacles, thus reaching major goals of the symposium e.g. international exchange and civil diplomacy.

Academic Theme

There are two main themes of our 2016 IVSA Symposium : “Small Animal Medicine” and the “Traditional Chinese Veterinary Medicine” which is peculiar to Chinese ethnics. By two sections including three courses, we hope that the delegates can acquire the basic concepts、core theoris and primary operational rules of these two categories as well as knowledge in addition to our textbooks.

Practice of Veterinary Acupuncture and Herbology
Theme 2
